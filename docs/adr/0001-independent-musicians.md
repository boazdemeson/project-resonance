# ADR-0001: Focus on Independent Musicians

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-01

---

## Context

The product initially aimed to serve musicians in general.

However, building for every type of musician would make the MVP too broad and increase development complexity.

A narrower audience allows faster iteration and stronger product-market fit.

---

## Decision

The MVP will focus on independent musicians and small collaborators.

This includes:

- Solo artists
- Singer-songwriters
- Producers
- Beat makers
- Composers
- Small bands

The MVP will not target:

- Record labels
- Enterprise studios
- Music distributors

---

## Alternatives Considered

### All musicians

Pros

- Larger potential audience

Cons

- Too many workflows
- Difficult prioritization
- Slower MVP

### Music producers only

Pros

- Extremely focused

Cons

- Excludes many creators who face the same collaboration problems

---

## Consequences

### Positive

- Clear messaging
- Easier feature prioritization
- Simpler UX
- Faster development

### Negative

- Enterprise features delayed
- Smaller initial audience

---

## Notes

Future versions may expand to larger teams and organizations after validating the independent musician workflow.
