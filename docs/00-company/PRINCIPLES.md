# Company Principles

These principles guide every product and engineering decision.

If a proposed feature violates one of these principles, we redesign it.

---

## 1. Creativity Comes First

The platform must never interrupt creativity.

Reduce decisions.

Reduce clicks.

Reduce friction.

---

## 2. Sessions Are Sacred

A Session is the source of truth.

Everything belongs to the Session.

Nothing fragments its history.

---

## 3. Never Lose Work

Creative work is irreplaceable.

Snapshots should never overwrite previous work.

History is valuable.

---

## 4. Solo First

The platform must be genuinely useful for musicians working alone.

Collaboration enhances the experience.

It is never required.

---

## 5. Collaboration Without Chaos

Every discussion belongs to the music.

No feedback should live in disconnected chats.

Context should never be lost.

---

## 6. Simplicity Wins

Simple products are harder to build.

Hide complexity.

Expose clarity.

---

## 7. Technology Should Be Invisible

Users should think about music.

Not software.

---

## 8. Respect Existing Workflows

Musicians already use DAWs they love.

We do not replace them.

We connect them.

---

## 9. Build for the Long Term

Prefer maintainability over shortcuts.

Prefer clarity over cleverness.

Prefer trust over growth hacks.

---

## 10. Solve Real Problems

Every feature must answer at least one question:

Does this make creating music easier?

Does this make continuing a Session easier?

Does this make collaboration easier?

If not, it probably doesn't belong.
