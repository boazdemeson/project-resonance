# Manifesto

## Why We Exist

Music deserves better than folders.

Every day, independent musicians create incredible ideas.

A melody recorded on a phone.

A guitar riff saved as "idea_final_v2.wav".

A late-night vocal take.

Most of these ideas never disappear because they are bad.

They disappear because they become lost.

Lost in folders.

Lost in cloud storage.

Lost in chats.

Lost in time.

Music creation should feel creative—not administrative.

Musicians shouldn't have to remember where Version 12 is.

They shouldn't search through WhatsApp to understand why a change was made.

They shouldn't rename files just to stay organized.

Creative work deserves a home.

Not a folder.

Not a chat.

A place where every creative Session grows naturally.

Where every Snapshot preserves a moment.

Where every note tells part of the story.

Where every decision stays connected to the music.

We believe technology should disappear into the background.

The musician should think about music.

The platform should think about everything else.

We are not building storage.

We are not building project management.

We are building the creative home for music.

One Session at a time.

---

## What Success Looks Like

When musicians finish creating,

they shouldn't wonder where anything is.

They should simply create.

Everything else should already be taken care of.
