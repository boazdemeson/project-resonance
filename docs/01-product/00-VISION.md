# Vision

## Mission

Empower independent musicians with a collaborative workspace where every song, revision, and conversation lives in one place.

## Vision

We believe making music should feel creative—not chaotic.

Independent musicians shouldn't have to manage projects through scattered folders, messaging apps, cloud drives, and emails. Every song deserves a home where ideas evolve naturally, collaborators stay aligned, and every revision tells the story of the creative process.

Our goal is to become the workspace musicians open every time they create together.

## Core Beliefs

- Songs are the center of every project.
- Collaboration should feel effortless.
- Version history should be automatic.
- Feedback should happen in context.
- The product should stay simple and focused.
- Independent musicians deserve professional tools regardless of budget.

## Long-Term Vision

Become the default collaboration platform for independent musicians worldwide.

## Decisions

- The product exists to empower independent musicians.
- The product should remain simple and musician-first.
- Professional collaboration tools should be accessible regardless of budget.
