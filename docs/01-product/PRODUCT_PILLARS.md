# Product Pillars

These principles guide every product decision.

---

## 1. Creativity First

The platform should never interrupt the creative process.

Every interaction should help musicians create rather than manage software.

---

## 2. Session Is Everything

A Session is the heart of the platform.

Every important piece of information belongs to a Session.

---

## 3. Preserve Everything

Creative work should never be lost.

Snapshots, notes, comments, and ideas remain accessible.

---

## 4. Solo First

Every feature should provide value to musicians working alone.

Collaboration should feel like an enhancement rather than a requirement.

---

## 5. Collaboration Without Chaos

When collaboration happens, every discussion, revision, and decision stays connected to the Session.

No scattered conversations.

No missing context.

---

## 6. Simplicity Above All

Powerful features should feel effortless.

Complexity belongs in the system, not in the user experience.

---

## 7. Music Before Files

The platform manages creative work—not folders.

Users should think about Sessions, not file structures.
