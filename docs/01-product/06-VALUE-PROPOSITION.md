# Value Proposition

## Our Promise

Music creation should feel creative—not chaotic.

We provide musicians with a workspace where every Session, Snapshot, idea, lyric, note, and conversation lives together.

No scattered folders.

No lost revisions.

No forgotten ideas.

Just one place where music evolves naturally.

---

# The Problem We Solve

Independent musicians use multiple disconnected tools to finish one song.

A typical collaboration might involve:

- DAW
- Google Drive
- WhatsApp
- Discord
- Email
- Notes

As projects grow, creative work becomes harder to manage.

Important feedback gets buried.

Versions become confusing.

Files get duplicated.

The creative process becomes fragmented.

---

# Our Solution

Instead of organizing files...

We organize songs.

Every song has its own workspace containing:

- Revisions
- Audio files
- Collaborators
- Comments
- Timeline
- Decisions

The song becomes the center of everything.

---

# Why We're Different

Cloud storage platforms manage files.

We manage the creative journey.

Messaging apps organize conversations.

We organize collaboration around the music.

Project management tools organize tasks.

We organize the evolution of a song.

---

# What Users Gain

- Confidence that the latest revision is always clear.
- Faster collaboration.
- Feedback that stays connected to the correct version.
- A complete history of every song.
- Less time managing files.
- More time making music.

---

# Our Positioning

We are not another cloud storage platform.

We are the collaborative workspace built specifically for independent musicians.

---

# Our North Star

Become the place musicians open whenever they work on a song.

## Decisions

- Primary audience: Independent musicians.
- The song is the core entity of the product.
- The product is positioned as a workspace, not cloud storage.
- The primary value is reducing collaboration chaos.
