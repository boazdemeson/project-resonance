# ADR-0005: The Song Is the Product

## Decision

The platform focuses on the creation and evolution of Songs.

Organization and distribution features are intentionally secondary.

Every feature should help musicians create, iterate, remember, and collaborate on Songs.

Projects, releases, and publishing are outside the primary scope of the platform.

## Consequences

The Song becomes the primary business entity.

Every new feature must answer:

"Does this improve the experience of creating a Song?"
