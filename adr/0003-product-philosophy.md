# ADR-0003: Build a Workspace, Not Cloud Storage

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-01

---

## Context

Existing cloud storage platforms already provide file synchronization and sharing.

Competing directly on storage offers little differentiation.

---

## Decision

The platform will position itself as a collaborative workspace for songs.

Storage exists to support collaboration rather than being the primary feature.

---

## Consequences

### Positive

- Stronger product identity
- Easier positioning
- More room for future collaboration features

### Negative

- Requires educating users on the product's unique approach
